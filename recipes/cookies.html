Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Description

This chocolate chip cookie recipes is going to know your socks off. You've never tasted anything better than this and once you try it I'l put down money syaing you're gonna tell everyone you know what a wonderful expereince it was!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of warm butter
  • 3/4 cups of sugar
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/4 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp of salt

Steps!

  1. Mix eggs, flour, and VE in a bowl.
  2. Add baking soda, salt and butter. (Mix well)
  3. Add 1 cup of chocolate chips and mix!
  4. Preheat oven to 350F
  5. Bake cookies for 10-12 minutes
  6. ENJOY!
